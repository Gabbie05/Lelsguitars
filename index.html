About Lels’ Guitar

Lel’s Guitar was created to celebrate the memory of Eleni (“Lels”) and our shared passion for music. Every instrument is handmade in our Abu Dhabi workshop—nothing is outsourced to CNC machines, and each guitar is lovingly shaped and finished by hand. Independent luthiers bring passion, knowledge and individual attention to each instrument, ensuring greater freedom for personalization compared with factory‑produced guitars[2].

We believe in giving back: 25 % of profits are donated to Teenage Cancer Trust, a UK charity that improves the cancer experience of young people aged 13–24 [1].
